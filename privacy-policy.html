1. Information We Collect

Transport Invoice app collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Company and customer information you enter
  • Invoice data and transportation details
  • App usage preferences and settings

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Creating and managing transportation invoices
  • Generating PDF and Excel reports
  • Saving your work locally on your device

3. Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using browser local storage. We do not transmit, store, or access your data on external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

This app may use third-party libraries for PDF generation and Excel export. These libraries process data locally and do not transmit information externally.

5. Data Security

Your data security is important to us. All data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted over the internet unless you explicitly choose to email or share invoices.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data
  • Delete your stored data
  • Export your data in various formats

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@transportinvoice.com
